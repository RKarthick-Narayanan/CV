About Me

I am a linguist and archivist with extensive experience in the documentation and preservation of lesser-known languages of India.

Doctor of Philosophy, Linguistics, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, India (2014-2021)

Master of Philosophy, Linguistics, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, India (2012-2014)

Master of Arts, Linguistics, Jawaharlal Nehru University, New Delhi, India (2010-2012)

Research Interests

  • Sociolinguistics
  • Documentation and Description of Lesser-Known Languages of India
  • Language Maintenance and Revitalization
  • Language Archiving

Publications

  • Monograph: Multlinguality, Vitality and Endangerment: Insights from the Lived Experiences of Multilingual India, Cambridge Scholars Publishing (In Preparation).
  • Journal: Assessing Language Endangerment: A Methodological Review, Language in India, 2019.
